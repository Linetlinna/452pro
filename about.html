About us

EF is a household name in Australia, with its origins dating back to 1974 when it first started operations in East Keillor, Victoria. From its humble beginnings in a storefront, EF has grown into a retail icon that adheres to a straightforward and profound philosophy: to provide professional-grade goods and services at the most competitive prices in the country. The foundation of the company is built on the belief that quality should not be a luxury but a necessity for every Australian consumer.

The business was sold in 1983 and nine more stores opened by 1999. In 2000, EF was acquired with the aim of spreading the successful model across the country.
